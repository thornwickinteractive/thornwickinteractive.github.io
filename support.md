# Fold Support

Have a question, found a bug, or want to send feedback?

Email us at **thornwickinteractive@gmail.com**

We aim to respond within seven days.

## Common questions

**Did my purchase process correctly?**
Purchases are handled by Apple. Open the App Store app on your device,
tap your profile icon, scroll to the in-app purchase you made, and Apple
will show the receipt. If something looks wrong, Apple's purchase support
at https://support.apple.com/billing handles refund requests.

**My game state disappeared.**
Fold stores all game state locally on your device. Reinstalling the app
or wiping iOS data resets it. We don't have a backup on our end. If
you're seeing this happen without a reinstall, email us with a description
and we'll investigate.

**How do I restore my Fold Pro purchase on a new device?**
Open Fold, tap any locked feature to bring up the paywall, and tap
"Restore Purchases." Sign in with the same Apple ID you used originally.
